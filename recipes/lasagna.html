Lasagna

Description

Lasagna (or lasagne) is a classic Italian dish consisting of layers of wide, flat pasta sheets alternated with rich fillings like meat sauce (ragù or bolognese), béchamel sauce, and various cheeses (including ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan), then baked. It is a versatile and beloved comfort food worldwide.

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Sauce
  • Cheese filling

Steps

  1. Prepare the sauces and cheese mixture.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Assemble in layers.
  4. Bake.
  5. Rest.
